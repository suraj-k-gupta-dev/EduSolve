Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Last revised: Added Gemini API disclosure and clarified data handling practices.

EduSolve respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of all users, including children, young students, and adults. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and mobile application (together, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

  • 1.1 AI Query Data
    Questions and inputs you submit to our AI engine are temporarily stored to generate responses. We do not retain or share this data beyond improving Service quality.
  • 1.2 Device and Technical Data
    We gather device type and operating system to optimize performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • 1.3 Third-Party AI Service (Google Gemini API)
    When you submit homework questions, EduSolve sends your questions to Google's Gemini API to generate step-by-step solutions. Google may process this data according to its own privacy policy at https://ai.google.dev/terms. EduSolve does not retain question data after the API response is displayed to you. We recommend reviewing Google's terms for details on their data practices.

1.4 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require user accounts, login, or personal identification.
  • We do not collect location data, phone numbers, or email addresses.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs (Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.).
  • We do not display advertisements or use ad networks.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IDFA, or Android Advertising ID for tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Improve the Service by analyzing anonymized usage patterns
  • Analyze usage trends and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Parents or guardians may request deletion of a child’s data by contacting us at the address below.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
  • We may disclose data to comply with legal requirements or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure servers) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • No Account Required: EduSolve does not use user accounts or login systems. All features are available immediately upon app launch.
  • No Tracking: The app does not use analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics) or ad networks to track your behavior.
  • No Persistent Data: Questions and responses are not saved to user profiles. Each session is independent.
  • Contact for Rights: For data deletion or inquiries, contact us at suraj.k.gupta.dev@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

AI Query Data: Your homework questions are sent to Google Gemini API and deleted immediately after the solution is generated. EduSolve servers do not store your questions or responses.

Device Data: Technical logs (OS version, app crashes) are retained for up to 30 days for debugging, then deleted.

No Account Data: Since EduSolve has no user accounts, we do not retain login information or user profiles.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised date at the top and notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
